Frontal Shoulder Press

Shoulder Exercises

Muscles:

  • Primary Movers:
    • Anterior Delt
    • Lateral Delt
  • Secondary Movers:
    • Triceps Lateral & Medial Head

ROM Leverages:

Both the Anterior Delt and the Lateral Delt gain leverage as degrees of humeral abduction increase.
Therefore choosing a variaton that has either a ascending or even resistance profile will be superior to a descending one.
