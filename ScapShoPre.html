Scapular Shoulder Press

Shoulder Exercises

Muscles:

  • Primary Movers:
    • Anterior Delt
    • Upper Chest
    • Lateral Delt
  • Secondary Movers:
    • Triceps Lateral & Medial Head

ROM Leverages:

Both the Anterior Delt and the Lateral Delt gain leverage as degrees of humeral abduction increase. While the pectoral muscles
gain leverage in the opposite direction. Therefore having an even or ascending resistance profile will put a focus on the Delts,
While a descending one will put a focus on the clavicular pecs.
